About Us

About SME

Social Micro Finance is an Islamic economic-based investment company in Bangladesh. Its aim is to work for implementing the 17 Sustainable Development Goals, increasing the purchasing power of customers, controlling the inflation rate, and establishing a sustainable investment policy worldwide.

Social Micro Finance started pilot operation on Thursday 30-July-2020 AD and 8-ZilHaj, 1441 Hijri. On 20-July-2023, Thursday, 1-Muharram-1445 Hijri, Prof. Dr. Mohammad Mahbubur Rahman and Muid Khan inaugurated the program at Jhenaidah Baitun Noor Jame Mosque.

This company is the reflection of the research jointly made by Dr. Muhammad Mahbubur Rahman & Muid Khan.

Our Vision

The vision of the company is to establish an Islamic and ethical transparent investment system, achieving the 17 Sustainable Development Goals. Additionally, it seeks proper utilization of solar energy in an environmentally friendly manner with prosperous human resources, society, and economy.


Our Mission

The mission of the company is to establish the "Qarrj Hasana", which is an interest-free loan system according to the Holy Quran, and also establish “Peace, Justice, and Strong Institutions” in alignment with the United Nations SDG-16.
