Our Services

Qaarj Hasana
This is a Mosque-based program where an account holder can make a monthly savings of BDT 30, and seek for interest-free loan as Qarrj Hasana, which can exceed their existing savings.
Zakat
This program is also a mosque-based one where people with financial capability will deposit their Zakat amount in the mosque fund, and it will be distributed equally among the needy people.
Islamic Employment
Training and investment will be arranged for the entrepreneurs, making a sustainable interest-free deal with the investors.
Skill Assesment
This program will empower youth with the post-training assessment.
